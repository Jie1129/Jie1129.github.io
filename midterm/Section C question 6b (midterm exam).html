Recipe Cooking Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves


Cooking Step

First

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant.
  2. Add in chicken.
  3. Continue to saute until chicken releases oil.

Second

  1. Add water,tamarind paste,curry leaves,potato,onion,suger and salt.
  2. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked throuhly.

Lastly

  1. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  2. Remove san serve.
